ABOUT JESUS CULTURE


Started at 6th of January 2013, a fellowship established by one family, with only 6 people decided to support the vision that the Lord impressed on the heart of Pastor Ronie. He was called to be a pastor of 6 people when it started.

The ministry of the Lord grow much bigger that all the members agreed to outsource and move out to a new place nearby. And it was first named Divine Grace Christian Community Ministries International and changed to
Light of Hope Christian Fellowship a year later.

With the divine intervention of the Lord, the church growth was so fast and a large amount of souls are saved on months went by. The church move on much bigger and wider place because of the numbers of soul added every week.

After 2 years of journey of the ministry of Christ Jesus, Pastor Ronie received a dream and vision from God to let his Brother-In-Law and Half Sister took over the existing ministry and started an outreached church, named after the last one
which is called Light of Hope World Conquest.

At the year of 21st of April 2016, Jesus Culture World Conquest Inc. was recognized by SEC and authorized to used it as a non-profit organization and it was well known as JESUS CULTURE PHILIPPINES or JESUS CULTURE CHURCH. 

And now large numbers of new souls are accepting our Lord Jesus Christ through G12 Strategy (Win, Consolidate, Disciple, and Send). The vision within the church helps us to be established and continued the race and equipping and training in our church. We are now part of Christ bigger movement. We are G12 Family.



PS. RONIELO R. TUMACAY
